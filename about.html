THE VAMAS STORY

Every blouse tells
a story.

Founded with a deep love for Indian craftsmanship, Vamas was born from a simple belief — every woman deserves a blouse that tells her story. We work directly with skilled karigars across India to bring you heirloom-quality pieces at honest prices.

Our name, Vamas, is rooted in the Sanskrit tradition — it means “beautiful” and “belonging to the left side,” a sacred direction in Indian culture. Each stitch we make is a tribute to the artisans whose hands carry centuries of knowledge.

From the looms of Varanasi to the block-print studios of Jaipur, every Vamas blouse is a journey across India’s rich textile heritage.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Values

Three principles guide everything we create, from the studio to your wardrobe.

Craftsmanship
Every piece is handmade with care by master karigars. No shortcuts, no compromises — only the finest finishing that stands the test of time.
Sustainability
We source fabrics ethically, support fair-trade practices, and ensure every artisan in our network earns a dignified livelihood.
Authenticity
We source directly from artisan communities, preserving traditional techniques and ensuring every design is genuinely handcrafted in India.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones That
Define Us

From a small Jaipur studio to India’s trusted blouse brand — here is how we grew.

2018
Founded in Jaipur with just 10 designs and a single karigar workshop. The first collection sold out in 3 weeks.
2020
Expanded to 200+ designs and launched our online store, bringing handcrafted blouses to customers across India.
2022
Crossed 5,000 happy customers and partnered with artisan clusters in Varanasi, Lucknow, and Bhopal.
2024
Featured in Vogue India’s “Brands Celebrating Indian Craft” spotlight. A proud moment for our entire karigar community.
2026
10,000+ customers, 500+ designs, and shipping to 15+ states. The journey continues — one blouse at a time.
